How It Works

1. Upload Your Resume

Provide your resume in PDF or DOCX format safely and securely.

2. Select Your Target Role

Choose the role you are applying for from our dropdown menu so we can match it against industry-standard requirements.

3. Discover Skill Gaps

Our engine automatically calculates an ATS compatibility score and identifies the critical skills you might be missing.

4. Actionable AI Feedback

Receive clear, punchy bullet points advising exactly on what to improve before you apply.
